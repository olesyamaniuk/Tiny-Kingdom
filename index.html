About This Game

Northern Tale 6 is a strategic time-management game where players guide heroic Vikings through challenges to restore their kingdom. From battling mythical creatures to rebuilding villages, the game combines engaging storytelling with intricate gameplay mechanics.

How to Play

  • Plan Your Moves:

    Manage time and resources to complete objectives efficiently.

  • Battle Enemies:

    Protect your kingdom from mythical creatures and invaders.

  • Rebuild the Land:

    Restore villages, bridges, and farms to their former glory.

  • Gather Resources:

    Collect wood, food, and gold to support your people.

  • Unlock Abilities:

    Upgrade heroes and unlock special powers to overcome tougher challenges.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing Northern Tale 6?

    Download the game from the Play Store, and follow the story to begin your adventure.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, with optional in-app purchases for faster progression and special items.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, the game supports offline play for uninterrupted gameplay.

  • What makes this game unique?

    A compelling mix of storytelling, strategy, and resource management set in a rich Viking world.

  • Are there regular updates?

    Yes, new levels and challenges are added regularly to keep the gameplay fresh.
